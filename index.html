Biography

I am currently an associate professor at China University of Geosciences(Beijing). I received the Bachelor Degree in Computer science and technology from Beijing Jiaotong University in 2007, and the Ph.D. degrees in Computer software and theory from SKLCS, Institute of Software, Chinese Academy of Sciences in 2013. I was a visiting scholar at IRIF Laboratory of Universite Paris Cite (University Paris Diderot - Paris 7) from 2017 to 2018.
