Biography

I am currently an associate professor at China University of Geosciences(Beijing). I received the Bachelor Degree in Computer science and technology from Beijing Jiaotong University in 2007, and the Ph.D. degrees in Computer science from SKLCS, Institute of Software, Chinese Academy of Sciences in 2013.
