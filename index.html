Biography

I am currently an associate professor at Shenzhen University. I received the Bachelor Degree in Computer science and technology from Sun Yat-sen University in 2007, and the Ph.D. degrees in Computer science from IRIF(PPS), University Paris Diderot - Paris 7 and SKLCS, Institute of Software, Chinese Academy of Sciences, under the joint cultivation, in 2013.

Research Interests

  • Program Analysis and Verification, Type Systems, Software Security, Automata Theory and Logic, and Machine Learning.
